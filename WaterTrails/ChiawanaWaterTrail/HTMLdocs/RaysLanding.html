Chiawana Water Trail

Ray's Landing
River Mile 347.5

Ray's Landing is a small grassy area easily visible from the river. The USACE owns the land from the river up to the road and this is open to the public. Ray owns the land above the road and hiking is permitted by permission from the owner. His phone number can be found on the gate just north of the landing. Ray purchased the land for the preservation value and he has shown great commitment to preserving the area from unwarranted development. This area and points south are highly unstable and the ground is constantly sluffing. A number of springs sprout from the hill side and these are entirely fed by irrigation of the farmlands on the flats above the cliff. This is a lovely grassy spot with modest slope down to the river's edge. We give this a WAR-2 rating as the grass and up-hill slope make rolling a wheelchair a bit of a chore.
