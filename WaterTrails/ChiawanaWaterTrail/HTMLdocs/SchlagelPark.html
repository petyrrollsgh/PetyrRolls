Chiawana Water Trail

Schlagel Park
River Mile 328

Schlagel Park is in a secluded backwater and protected from the wind. It has a public cement boat ramp, dock and restrooms. The facilities were recently upgraded. It is also home to Columbia Marine Center. However, except for the ramp, the shoreline is inaccessible. While the shoreline is not very friendly to paddlers, the east end has an old dock and might be a good area to try paddling for absolute beginners.

We give Schlagel Park a WAR-2 rating as the cement boat ramp, as is usual with many boat ramps, is steep and bumpy.
