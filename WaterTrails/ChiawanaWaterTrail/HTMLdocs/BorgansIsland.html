Borgan's Island
River Miles 331

Just down river from Chiawana Park is Borganâ€™s Island (or Chiawana Island). It is closed to public use so please do not land and walk around. However, it is an excellent site for observing birds and maybe other wildlife. Just down stream is a second unnamed island that is also fun to paddle around.

These islands are a good example of why the shoreline needs protecting. Being in a central location, the islands were good places to stop and have a picnic. But unfortunately, that idyllic concept soon turns into large gatherings, lost of drinking, burning of every available piece of wood including live trees, dozens of blooms of white toilet paper and just plain trash.
