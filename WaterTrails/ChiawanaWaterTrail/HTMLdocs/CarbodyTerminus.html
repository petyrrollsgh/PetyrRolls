Chiawana Water Trail

Carbody Beach (RM 346) and the Columbia River Road Terminus (RM 345.5)

Carbody Beach is a popular motorized boating and partying spot during the summer months. It is probably best to avoid this area when in heavy use. There are however some quieter and shadier spots just downstream including the Columbia River Road Terminus. The road is gated about Â½ mile below the Carbody Beach and provides relatively easy access to the river. It is a good spot for put-in or take-out. There is adequate parking with a relatively short trail to the water. Due to the sandy nature of the trail, the access has a WAR-2 rating.
