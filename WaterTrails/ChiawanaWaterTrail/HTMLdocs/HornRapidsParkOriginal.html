Tapteal Water Trail

Horn Rapids Park
River Mile 19.7

Horn Rapids Park is located about 1/4 mile from Wanawish Dam and is an excellent takeout for a paddle from the Benton City boat ramp. Camping is/was allowed here for the summer months, but is closed during winter. There appears to be some concern that Benton County may close the park entirely.


Our Parks - Benton County WA

While the campground has restroom facilities, these are not near the boat ramp and with closure there may not be any restrooms available.

The cement slab ramp is in decent shape with a modest slope. It is adequate for all human-powered craft as well as small motor craft. The ramp has plenty of paved parking, but not much shade or wind protection. We give this a WAR-1 rating, and persons with disabilities will find this launch friendlier than most others

HRP should be a takeout point only as all donwstream access sites are below the dangerous Wanawish dam
