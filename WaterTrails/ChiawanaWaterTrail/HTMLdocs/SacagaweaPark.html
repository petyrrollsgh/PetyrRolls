Chiawana Water Trail

Sacagawea Park and Boat Launch
River Miles 326.5 to 325


×

Sacagawea Park is an obvious terminus of a paddle from Chiawana Park or Wade Park. The river is big and slow through this section and it is quite possible to paddle back up river to your point of origin. However, There are a few sections where emergency takeouts could be tricky. Please check the weather for high winds or rain. If in doubt hug the shoreline.

The park has plenty of amenities including primitive camping as part of the Discovery Water Trail. It is not clear where these campsites are located. It is also home to the Sacajawea Interpretive Center. A Discover Pass is required for use. The Park is closed in the winter months but the boat ramp is separate from the park and is open year-round.

We give the Park and the boat ramp WAR-2 ratings as the sandy swimming beach is too soft for wheelchair use, the boat ramps are too steep and bumpy for safe rolling and the docks are not amenable to low profile water craft.
