Tapteal Water Trail

West Richland Launch
River Mile: 9.4

The launch is on the northeast corner of the bridge over to West Richland. Parking is limited and there are no facilities. Hyde Road Launch is just upstream and is a much better access point.

We give this a WAR-2 rating as the descent to the river is modestly steep and undeveloped. Again, Hyde Road is much preferable.
