Tapteal Water Trail

Snively, Hyde and Duportail
River Miles 17.2, 9.7, 6.5 Respectively

Snively Road, Hyde Road and Duportail Road water trail access points are all Washington Department of Fish and Wildlife facilities. Hence, they require a Discover Pass. All have portable toilets and good parking as well as a primitive gravel boat launch.

They are excellent places to start or end a paddling trip. The trip from Snively launch to the Duportail launch or West Richland launch is a popular float with the hot-summer day inner-tube crowd. Things can get quite rowdy. Choose a weekday or cool season paddle for a more serene experience.

All three launches are rated WAR-1 as the gravel launches are not steep and provide a good place to enter and exit your water craft.
