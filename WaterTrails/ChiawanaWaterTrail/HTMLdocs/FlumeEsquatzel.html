Chiawana Water Trail

The Flume (Potholes Canal) (RM 345) and Esquatzel Wasteway (RM 343.5)

These irrigation discharge points are two larger wasteway operated by the South Columbia Basin Irrigation Project. Both canals overhang the river and form short waterfalls surrounded by a small basin. It is not recommended and probably trespassing to enter the canals and the falling water could be hazardous. Nevertheless, the surrounding land is open to the public and both sites are popular for swimming and fishing.

There are no developed trails leading to the water and carrying paddle craft to and from the beach requires some effort. These are not wheelchair friendly options and we give these a WAR-3 rating because of the brush and rocks and other obstacles that may be encountered.
