Tapteal Water Trail

Upper Horn Rapids and Omaha Beach
River Miles 24 and 21.5 respectively


×

These two small beaches on River Left are nice places to stop and relax, but are not very suitable as put-ins or take-outs. They are reached by a sandy road from S.R. 225 and do not have signage along the road. There is no official parking, facilities or other amenities, but they do provide an emergency exit with a walk to S.R. 225

We give these a WAR-2 rating because of the sand.
