As with all instrumentation, a stable and quiet power supply is required. I spent a great deal of time studying Switch Mode Power Supplies (SMPS) and eventually decided on a Fly-back configuration. For the Universal Sensor Interface (USI) project, I needed a power supply capable of powering op-amps with both +12V and -12V rails as well as +5V and might as well have +3.3V too.
